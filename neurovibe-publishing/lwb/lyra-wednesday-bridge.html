Lyra and the Wednesday Bridge

A gentle NeuroVibe Story about brave mornings, supportive helpers, and crossing life’s bridges one step at a time.

The Story

Some mornings can feel overwhelming, especially when the day ahead feels uncertain.

Lyra and the Wednesday Bridge tells the story of a young girl learning to cross difficult moments with the help of caring adults, patient support, and a loyal helper dog named Mochi.

Through gentle storytelling and calming imagery, the book explores emotional regulation, transitions, and the quiet courage children show every day.
